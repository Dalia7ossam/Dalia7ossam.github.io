About

Dalia approaches fashion as a conversation between aesthetics, function and detail. Her work is shaped by a steady curiosity — for new techniques, new silhouettes, and the small decisions that make a garment feel considered rather than generic.

She is currently completing her degree at the Faculty of Applied Arts, Fashion Design Department, Helwan University, where her training spans garment construction, digital design and presentation. Alongside her studies, she founded Dibaj, an independent fashion label, and recently presented her own work on the runway at Cairo Fashion Show 2026.
